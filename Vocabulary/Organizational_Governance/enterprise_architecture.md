## One-Sentence Summary
Enterprise Architecture is the holistic design of an organization's structure, processes, technologies, and data—ensuring alignment and enabling effective strategy execution.

## The Core Idea
- **Holistic design:** Designs all organizational elements together
- **Alignment:** Ensures alignment across organization
- **Integration:** Integrates business and technology
- **Future state:** Designs future state and transition

## How It Works
1. **Assess current:** Understand current state
2. **Design future:** Design desired future state
3. **Analyze gaps:** Identify gaps
4. **Plan transition:** Plan how to transition
5. **Guide execution:** Guide implementation

## Think of It Like This
Enterprise architecture is like **city planning**: Designs streets, buildings, utilities; ensures they work together; guides development.

## The "So What?" Factor
- **Alignment:** Strategy, process, technology aligned
- **Efficiency:** Reduces redundancy and waste
- **Scalability:** Designed for growth
- **Understanding:** Understand how organization works

## Watch Out For
⚠️ **Too abstract:** May not be actionable.
⚠️ **Quickly outdated:** Architecture must be maintained.
