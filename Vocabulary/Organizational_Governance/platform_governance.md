## One-Sentence Summary
Platform Governance establishes policies and controls for platform organizations—managing the balance between platform owner interests and ecosystem participant interests.

## The Core Idea
- **Ecosystem:** Platform creates ecosystem of participants
- **Rules:** Platform sets rules for participants
- **Value:** Platform creates value for all
- **Balance:** Balance between platform owner and participants
- **Evolution:** Platform governance evolves

## How It Works
1. **Define platform strategy:** What is platform strategy?
2. **Set rules:** What rules govern platform?
3. **Manage ecosystem:** How do participants interact?
4. **Ensure fairness:** Rules fair to all participants
5. **Evolve:** Update rules as platform evolves

## Think of It Like This
Platform governance is like **market regulation:** Regulator sets rules; oversees market; ensures fair competition; protects participants.

## The "So What?" Factor
- **Ecosystem health:** Governance ensures ecosystem health
- **Participation:** Fair rules encourage participation
- **Innovation:** Governance enables innovation
- **Sustainability:** Governance ensures platform sustainability

## Watch Out For
⚠️ **Platform control:** Platform owner too controlling.
⚠️ **Participant exploitation:** Participants exploited by platform.
