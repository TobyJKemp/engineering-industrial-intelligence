## One-Sentence Summary
IT Governance establishes policies and processes for managing IT resources—ensuring technology investments align with business strategy and managing IT risks.

## The Core Idea
- **Strategy alignment:** IT strategy aligned with business
- **Investment management:** Managing IT investments
- **Risk management:** Managing IT-related risks
- **Performance:** Ensuring IT delivers value
- **Compliance:** Meeting regulatory requirements

## How It Works
1. **Define IT strategy:** How does IT support business?
2. **Set governance:** Policies and processes for IT
3. **Manage portfolio:** Manage IT investments
4. **Monitor performance:** Track IT performance
5. **Manage risk:** Identify and manage IT risks

## Think of It Like This
IT governance is like **capital budgeting:** Organizations decide what IT investments to make; manage those investments; ensure they deliver value.

## The "So What?" Factor
- **Alignment:** IT aligned with business
- **ROI:** Ensure IT investments deliver ROI
- **Risk:** Manage IT risks
- **Compliance:** Meet regulatory requirements

## Watch Out For
⚠️ **Over-control:** Too much governance stifles innovation.
⚠️ **Shadow IT:** Insufficient IT governance drives shadow IT.
